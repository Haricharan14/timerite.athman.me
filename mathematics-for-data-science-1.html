Important Information

Eligibility Criteria for Final Exam:

  • Average of best 5 out of first 9 weeks assignments ≥ 40/100
  • Attendance in at least one quiz

Final Score Calculation:

\[T = \underbrace{0.1 \times \text{GAA}}_{\text{assignments}} + \max\Bigg(\underbrace{0.6F + 0.2\max(\text{Qz1}, \text{Qz2})}_{\text{option 1}}, \: \underbrace{0.4F + 0.2\text{Qz1} + 0.3\text{Qz2}}_{\text{option 2}}\Bigg)\]

Where:

  • \(\text{GAA}\) = Average of best 10 out of first 11 weekly assignments
  • \(\text{Qz1}\) = Quiz 1 score
  • \(\text{Qz2}\) = Quiz 2 score
  • \(F\) = Final exam score

Note: Though Week 12 assignment score is not included in GAA, Week 12 contents will be included in the final exam.

Bonus Marks Information

Course Participation Marks

These marks are designed to encourage student participation in course activities.

⚠️ Important Notes:

  • Bonus marks are added ONLY for students who pass the course
  • These marks only impact the course grade, not the pass criteria
  • Marks can vary from course to course (range: 0-2)

Criteria for Bonus Marks:

  1. Mock Test Performance (2 marks):
    • Average of all mock tests before Quiz 1 & Quiz 2 ≥ 40/100
    • Mock tests must be completed within specified time
  2. Additional Activities (up to 3 marks):
    • Course team may set up additional activities
    • If no activities are set up, this bonus won't be applicable
