Score Calculation

Component Calculations

\[ \text{GAA} = \frac{\sum \text{Best 10 Weekly Assignments}}{10} \]
Average of First 10 Graded Assignments
\[ \text{BPTA} = \frac{\sum \text{Sum of 4 BPT}}{4} \]
Average of First 4 Graded Assignments

Final Score Formula

\begin{aligned} T = & \underbrace{0.10 \times \text{GAA}}_{\text{Graded Assignments}} + \underbrace{0.20 \times \text{Qz1}}_{\text{Quiz 1}} + \underbrace{0.30 \times \text{OPE}}_{\text{Online Proctored Exam}} + \\ & \underbrace{0.30 \times \text{F}}_{\text{Final Exam}} + \underbrace{0.10 \times \text{BPTA}}_{\text{Bonus Points for Timely Activities}} \end{aligned}

Where:

  • GAA: Average of score in Best 9 out of the 10 weekly graded assignments
  • BPTA: Average of the 4 BPT scores
  • Qz1: Score in Quiz I (0, if not attempted)
  • OPE: Score in online remote proctored programming exam
  • F: Score in End Term exam

  • Eligibility to attend the end term exam:
