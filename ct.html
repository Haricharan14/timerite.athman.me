Final Score Formula

\[ T = \underbrace{0.1 \times \text{GAA}}_{\text{10 marks}} + \max\Bigg( \underbrace{0.6F + 0.2\max(\text{Qz1}, \text{Qz2})}_{\text{option 1}}, \: \underbrace{0.4F + 0.2\text{Qz1} + 0.3\text{Qz2}}_{\text{option 2}} \Bigg) \]
Where:
  • GAA: Average of First 10 weekly assignments
  • F: Final Exam Score
  • Qz1: Quiz 1 Score (0 if not attempted)
  • Qz2: Quiz 2 Score (0 if not attempted)
Options:
  • Option 1: 60% Final + 20% Best Quiz
  • Option 2: 40% Final + 20% Quiz1 + 30% Quiz2
  • System automatically picks the maximum of the two options

Bonus Marks Information

These marks are designed to encourage student participation in course activities.

    • Course team may set up additional activities
