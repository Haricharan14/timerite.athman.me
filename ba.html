Score Calculation

Quiz Score Calculation

\[ \text{Quiz Score} = \underbrace{0.7 \times \max(\text{Qz1}, \text{Qz2})}_{\text{best quiz}} + \underbrace{0.3 \times \min(\text{Qz1}, \text{Qz2})}_{\text{lower quiz}} \]
Must score at least 8 in one quiz and 5 in the other
Total quiz score (QZ) must be ≥ 7/20

Assignment Score Calculation

\[ A = \underbrace{\text{Sum of best 2 assignments}}_{\text{out of 3 assignments}} \]
Each assignment worth 20 marks
Must submit at least 1 of first 2 assignments

Final Exam (F)

Set for 45 marks, capped at 40
Must score ≥ 10/40 to pass

Final Score Formula

\[ \text{Total Score} = \underbrace{\text{Quiz Score}}_{\text{20 marks}} + \underbrace{A}_{\text{40 marks}} + \underbrace{F}_{\text{40 marks}} = 100 \text{ marks} \]
