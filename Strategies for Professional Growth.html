\begin{aligned} T = & \underbrace{0.15 \times \text{GAA}}_{\text{Graded Assignments}} + \underbrace{0.25 \times \text{GP}}_{\text{Programming Assignments}} + \underbrace{0.25 \times \text{Qz2}}_{\text{Quiz 2}} + & \underbrace{0.35 \times \text{F}}_{\text{Final Exam}} \end{aligned}
GAA: Average score in First 10 Graded Assignments
GP: score in Group Project (0, if not participated)
Qz2: score in Quiz II (0, if not attempted), based on weeks 1-7
F: score in final exam, based on weeks 1-12
