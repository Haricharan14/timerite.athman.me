Score Calculation

Component Calculations

\[ \text{GAA} = \frac{\sum \text{Best 4 of 7 Weekly Assignments}}{4} \]
Average of best 4 out of 7 weekly assignments
P1, P2 = 0.8 × Submission + 0.2 × Peer Review

Final Score Formula

\[ \begin{aligned} T = & \underbrace{0.15 \times \text{GAA}}_{\text{Weekly}} + \underbrace{0.2 \times \text{ROE1}}_{\text{Remote Exam}} + \underbrace{0.2 \times \text{P1} + 0.2 \times \text{P2}}_{\text{Projects}} + \underbrace{0.25 \times F}_{\text{Final}} \end{aligned} \]
Where:
  • T: Final Course Score
  • GAA: Graded Assignments Average (Best 4 of 7)
  • ROE1: Remote Online Exam Score
  • P1, P2: Project Scores (including peer reviews)
  • F: Final Exam Score

Note: Projects have two components - Submissions (80%) and Peer Reviews (20%)
