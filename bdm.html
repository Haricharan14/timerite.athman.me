Score Calculation

Graded Assignments (GA)

\[ \text{GA} = \frac{\sum \text{Best 3 out of 4 Assignments}}{3} \]
Each assignment worth 10 marks
Total contribution: 30% of final score

Final Score Formula

\[ T = \underbrace{0.3 \times \text{GA}}_{\text{30 marks}} + \underbrace{0.2 \times \text{Q2}}_{\text{20 marks}} + \underbrace{0.2 \times \text{ROE}}_{\text{20 marks}} + \underbrace{0.3 \times F}_{\text{30 marks}} = 100 \text{ marks} \]
Where:
  • GA: Best 3 out of 4 Graded Assignments
  • Q2: Quiz 2 Score
  • ROE: Remote Online Exam Score
  • F: Final Exam Score
