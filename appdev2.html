Score Calculation

Component Calculations

\[ \text{GAA1} = \frac{\sum \text{First 10 Weekly Assignments}}{10} \]
Average of first 10 weekly assignments
\[ \text{GAA2} = \frac{\text{Week 1 Programming} + \text{Week 2 Programming}}{2} \]
Average of programming assignments from weeks 1 & 2
Contributes 5% to final score

Final Score Formula

\[ T = \underbrace{0.05 \times \text{GAA1}}_{\text{weekly}} + \underbrace{0.05 \times \text{GAA2}}_{\text{programming}} + \max \begin{cases} \underbrace{0.35F + 0.25\text{Qz1} + 0.3\text{Qz2}}_{\text{option 1}} \\ \underbrace{0.5F + 0.3\max(\text{Qz1}, \text{Qz2})}_{\text{option 2}} \end{cases} \]
Where:
  • GAA1: First 10 Graded assignments Average
  • GAA2: Programming Assignments Average
  • Qz1: Quiz 1 Score
  • Qz2: Quiz 2 Score
  • F: Final Exam Score
