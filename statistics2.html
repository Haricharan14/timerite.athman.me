\[ \text{GAA} = \frac{\sum \text{First 10 Weekly Assignments}}{10} \]

Final Score Formula

\[ \begin{aligned} T = & \underbrace{0.1 \times \text{GAA}}_{\text{Weekly Assignments}} + \max\left\{ \begin{aligned} & \underbrace{0.6 \times F + 0.2 \times \max(\text{Qz1}, \text{Qz2})}_{\text{Option 1}}, \\ & \underbrace{0.4 \times F + 0.2 \times \text{Qz1} + 0.3 \times \text{Qz2}}_{\text{Option 2}} \end{aligned} \right\} + \underbrace{\text{Bonus}}_{\text{Capped to 5}} \end{aligned} \]
Where:
  • T: Final Course Score
  • GAA: Graded Assignments Average
  • Qz1: Quiz 1 Score
  • Qz2: Quiz 2 Score
  • F: Final Exam Score

Note: Though Week 12 assignment score is not included in GAA, Week 12 contents will be included in the final exam.
