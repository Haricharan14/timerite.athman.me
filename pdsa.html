Score Calculation

Component Calculations

\[ \text{GAA} = \frac{\sum \text{Sum of First 10 Weekly Assignments}}{10} \]

Final Score Formula

\[T = \underbrace{0.1 \times \text{GAA}}_{\text{assignments}} + \underbrace{0.4 \times F}_{\text{final}} + \underbrace{0.2 \times \text{OP}}_{\text{prog exam}} \] \[+ \: \max\Big(\underbrace{0.2 \times \max(\text{Qz1}, \text{Qz2})}_{\text{option 1}}, \: \underbrace{0.15 \times \text{Qz1} + 0.15 \times \text{Qz2}}_{\text{option 2}}\Big)\]

Where:

  • \(\text{GAA}\) = Average score in First 10 weekly graded assignments
  • \(\text{Qz1}, \text{Qz2}\) = Quiz scores (0 if not attempted)
  • \(\text{OP}\) = Online Programming Exam score
  • \(F\) = Final exam score
