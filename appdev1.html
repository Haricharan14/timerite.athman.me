Score Calculation

Final Score Formula:

\[T = \underbrace{0.15 \times \text{GLA}}_{\text{lab}} + \underbrace{0.05 \times \text{GA}}_{\text{assignments}} + \] \[\max\Big(\underbrace{0.35F + 0.2\text{Qz1} + 0.25\text{Qz2}}_{\text{option 1}}, \underbrace{0.4F + 0.3\max(\text{Qz1}, \text{Qz2})}_{\text{option 2}}\Big)\]
  • \(\text{GLA}\) = Best 3 out of 6 lab assignments (Weeks 2-7)
  • \(\text{GA}\) = Average of first 10 objective assignments
  • \(\text{Qz1, Qz2}\) = Quiz scores
  • \(F\) = Final exam score
