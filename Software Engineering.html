Score Calculation

Component Calculations

\[ \text{GAA} = \frac{\sum \text{Sum of First 10 Weekly Assignments}}{10} \]

Final Score Formula

\[ \begin{align*} T &= \underbrace{0.05 \times \text{GAA}}_{\text{Assignments}} + \underbrace{0.2 \times \text{Qz2}}_{\text{Quiz 2}} + \underbrace{0.4 \times F}_{\text{Final Exam}} \\ &\quad + \underbrace{0.1 \times \text{GP1}}_{\text{Group Project 1-3}} + \underbrace{0.1 \times \text{GP2}}_{\text{Group Project 4-6}} + \underbrace{0.1 \times \text{PP}}_{\text{Presentation}} + \underbrace{0.05 \times \text{CP}}_{\text{Participation}} \end{align*} \]

Where:

  • \(\text{GAA}\) = Average of first 10 weekly assignments
  • \(\text{Qz2}\) = Quiz 2 score (0 if not attempted)
  • \(F\) = Final exam score
  • \(\text{GP1}\) = Group Project 1-3 score
  • \(\text{GP2}\) = Group Project 4-6 score
  • \(\text{PP}\) = Project Presentation score
  • \(\text{CP}\) = Course Participation score
