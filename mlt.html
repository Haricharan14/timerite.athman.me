Score Calculation

Component Calculations

\[ \text{GAA} = \frac{\sum \text{Sum of First 10 Weekly Assignments}}{10} \]

Final Score Formula

\[T = \underbrace{0.1 \times \text{GAA}}_{\text{Average of First 10 GAs}} + \underbrace{0.4F}_{\text{Final Exam}} \] \[+ \: \max\Big(\underbrace{0.25\text{Qz1} + 0.25\text{Qz2}}_{\text{option 1}}, \: \underbrace{0.4\max(\text{Qz1}, \text{Qz2})}_{\text{option 2}}\Big) + \underbrace{\text{Bonus}}_{\text{max 3}}\]

Where:

  • \(\text{GAA}\) = Average of first 10 weekly assignments
  • \(\text{Qz1}\) = Quiz 1 score (0 if not attempted)
  • \(\text{Qz2}\) = Quiz 2 score (0 if not attempted)
  • \(F\) = Final exam score

Note: Though Week 12 assignment score is not included in GAA, Week 12 contents will be included in the final exam.
