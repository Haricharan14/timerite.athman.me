Grading FAQs

OPE and ET Definitions

- OPE: Online Proctored Exam
- ET: End-Term Exam

What happens if I miss OPE?

  • If ET is taken with score ≥35: "I_OP" grade (OPE must be retaken)
  • If ET is not taken: "U" grade (entire course must be repeated)

What happens if I miss ET?

  • If OPE is taken with score >35/100 AND Python-DB is correct: "I" grade (ET must be retaken)
  • If either condition is not met: "U" grade (course must be repeated)

What if I miss both OPE and ET?

"U" grade will be awarded and the course must be repeated

When is "I_BOTH" grade given?

This applies when:

  • ET is absent
  • AND in OPE either:
    • Score is <35 OR
    • Python-DB question is incorrect

In this case, both ET and OPE must be retaken

What if I take both OPE and ET?

  • If Python-DB is correct: Final grade depends on total score (T)
  • If Python-DB is incorrect:
    • ET ≥40: "I_OP" (must retake OPE)
    • ET <40: "U" (must repeat course)
